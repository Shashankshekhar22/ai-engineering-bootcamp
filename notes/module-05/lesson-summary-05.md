💼 Customer Support & Success

1. Ticket Triage and Routing
   Problem: Manual categorization and routing of large volumes of incoming support tickets lead to delays and misassigned issues.
   Inputs: Ticket subject, body, and customer account tier.
   Returns: Assigned department, priority level (High/Medium/Low), and a suggested resolution category.
2. Sentiment and Churn Risk Analysis
   Problem: Identifying at-risk customers early requires continuously analyzing unstructured feedback across calls, emails, and surveys.
   Inputs: Customer interaction history, support chat logs, and NPS (Net Promoter Score) survey comments.
   Returns: Churn risk score (0-100), primary pain point, and an action item for the account manager.
   🚀 Sales & Marketing
3. Prospect Account Research
   Problem: Sales representatives spend significant time manually searching for news and background on target companies before outreach.
   Inputs: Target company name and domain URL.
   Returns: Recent news, executive leadership changes, funding history, and potential pain points that align with company products.
4. Dynamic Proposal Generator
   Problem: Customizing sales proposals for varying client needs is tedious and prone to human error or inconsistent branding.
   Inputs: Prospect profile, required services, pricing tier, and agreed-upon delivery timeline.
   Returns: A fully formatted proposal document or presentation outline tailored to the specific client.
5. Competitor Ad Campaign Monitor
   Problem: Tracking competitor movements in ad auctions requires manually combing through multiple platforms.
   Inputs: Target competitor's website, target keywords, and geographical region.
   Returns: Estimated ad spend, primary ad copy, and keyword targeting gaps.
   💰 Finance & Accounting
6. Contract Risk and Compliance Audit
   Problem: Reviewing hundreds of supplier or vendor contracts for unfavorable terms or regulatory compliance is labor-intensive.
   Inputs: Contract text (PDF or raw text) and the company's compliance policy rules.
   Returns: List of high-risk clauses, missing mandatory terms, and a recommended approval status.
7. Invoice Data Extraction
   Problem: Processing varying PDF invoice formats causes manual data entry bottlenecks and errors in accounting systems.
   Inputs: Invoice document (PDF/Image) or a scanned batch.
   Returns: Structured JSON data containing Vendor Name, Invoice Number, Issue Date, Due Date, and Total Amount.
   🏢 Human Resources & Operations
8. Resume Screening & Ranking
   Problem: HR teams are overwhelmed with high volumes of resumes when sourcing for open roles.
   Inputs: Job description requirements and a batch of candidate resumes (PDF/Text).
   Returns: Ranked list of candidates with justification scores, highlight of matched skills, and identified skill gaps.
9. Policy and Knowledge Base Query
   Problem: Employees waste valuable time searching for internal company policies, benefits, or SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures).
   Inputs: Employee query, employee role, and department.
   Returns: Direct, accurate answer based strictly on company handbooks, with the exact source document and page number cited.
